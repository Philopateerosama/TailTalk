About Luna

Luna is a beautiful 1-year-old black domestic shorthair with mesmerizing green eyes. She was found as a stray and brought to our shelter where she's been waiting for her forever home.

Luna is the perfect mix of playful and cuddly. She loves chasing toys but also enjoys curling up in your lap for naps. She's litter-trained, spayed, and gets along well with other cats.
