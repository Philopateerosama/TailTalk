Charlie

About Charlie

Charlie is a 4-year-old Chihuahua mix who is deaf but doesn't let that slow him down! He was surrendered when his owners moved overseas and couldn't take him.

Charlie responds well to hand signals and vibrations. He's house-trained, loves being carried in a doggy sling, and enjoys quiet cuddle sessions. Perfect for apartment living or someone looking for a portable companion.
